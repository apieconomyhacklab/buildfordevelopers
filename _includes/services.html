Main Book Themes

The book is built around three themes, each of which is one part of the book.

APIs as products

Learn why APIs should be seen as products and what it means in practise. We will use existing great productised APIs as examples to avoid only theoretical viewpoints.

Developer-first approach

We'll go through features and aspects on API development to focus on while keeping primary customer - application developer at the center. You will lean what kind of team you'll need to craft loves your customers will love.

Business to Developer marketing

In the last part we'll guide you towards successful business developer marketing (B2D). You'll learn how to get started and things to avoid.
